WHAT IS A RAIN GARDEN?

  • Rain gardens are places on the ground for native plants.
  • They capture extra rain that would run down the street or sidewalk.
  • Rain gardens absorb a few inches of water everytime it rains.
  • They conserve water too so you don't need to water a lawn every few days!
  • Rain gardens can be very colorful and popular.
