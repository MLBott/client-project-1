How Rain Gardens Help Us

  • They keep water from pooling nearby and causing damage to your home.
  • They prevent toxic water from running into the sewers, ponds, and lakes.
  • They reduce the chances of mosquitoes and other pests from hanging around puddles of water.
  • They help native plants stay around and continue growing.




  • Rain gardens keep the soil from drying out and breaking apart.
  • Air moisture stays more regular with them around which helps clouds form.
  • All these positive things would help a lot of people in your city.
  • And a better environment and happier neighbors means more time to play!
